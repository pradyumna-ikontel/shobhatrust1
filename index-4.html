Our Vision:

Shobha Trust envisions a world where cancer is prevented through community-driven initiatives, ensuring access to early detection and treatment.

Community-Centric Approach:

Implementing community-based cancer screening in Tumkur district, Karnataka, targeting underprivileged populations and utilizing advanced screening methods

Cultural Tailoring for Awareness:

Addressing disparities through culturally tailored educational initiatives, fostering awareness, and reducing stigma attached to cancer detection

Our Mission

Mission

We believe that we can save more lifes with you.

Prevention at the Core:

Focus on early detection and prevention through identifying high-risk groups and screening for oral, breast, cervix, stomach, and colorectal cancers.

Reducing Disparities:

Exploring avenues to reduce disparities in accessing cancer screening services, addressing factors such as awareness, socio-economic status, and cultural backgrounds.

Behavior Change Initiatives:

Implementing behavior change initiatives to address cancer risk factors, including role plays and in-person sessions, building advocacy for the program.

Volunteer

Ignite Change: Join Us as a Volunteer

At Shobha Trust, we believe that the strength of our impact lies in the collective efforts of compassionate individuals. If you are passionate about making a meaningful difference in the fight against cancer, we invite you to join us as a volunteer. Becoming a part of our volunteer community is more than just lending your time; it's about igniting change, fostering hope, and being a beacon of support for those facing the challenges of cancer. As a volunteer, you'll play a vital role in our community-based initiatives, from assisting in cancer screenings to spreading awareness and providing comfort to those undergoing treatment. Join hands with us, and together, let's create a ripple effect of positive change in the lives of those affected by cancer. Your commitment can make a profound impact, and we welcome you to be a catalyst for transformation in the journey towards a cancer-free future.
