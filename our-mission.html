Our Mission

Mission

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Prevention at the Core:

Focus on early detection and prevention through identifying high-risk groups and screening for oral, breast, cervix, stomach, and colorectal cancers.

Reducing Disparities:

Exploring avenues to reduce disparities in accessing cancer screening services, addressing factors such as awareness, socio-economic status, and cultural backgrounds.

Behavior Change Initiatives:

Implementing behavior change initiatives to address cancer risk factors, including role plays and in-person sessions, building advocacy for the program.

Our strategic priorities are:

  • Protecting charities from abuse or mismanagement
  • Enabling trustees to run their charities effectively
  • Encouraging greater transparency and accountability
Join With Us

Shobha Trust envisions a world where cancer is prevented through community-driven initiatives, ensuring access to early detection and treatment.
